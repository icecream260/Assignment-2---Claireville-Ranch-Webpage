Lessons



Western Horse Riding Lessons

Clairville Ranch offers Western riding lessons taught by certified instructors, providing a great opportunity for riders of all skill levels to learn. Whether you are new to riding or want to improve your existing skills, our programs are designed to suit a variety of ages and abilities. Our welcoming atmosphere and convenient location close to the city make it easy for anyone to begin their riding journey. We're dedicated to helping you gain confidence and master the art of Western riding.

Safe Rider Program

This four-week program is designed for beginners. You will learn how to safely groom, tack, and handle horses. Once you graduate from this program, you can enter our weekly lesson program.

  • Age Requirement: Minimum 10 years old
  • Rider Requirement: 27-inch inseam
  • Price: $400 per person

Weekly Riding Lessons

We offer group, semi-private, or private lessons. These lessons are for riders who have graduated from the Safe Rider Program or have prior experience.

  • Group, Semi-Private, or Private
  • Rider Certificates: OE Rider Certificates can be earned
  • Group Price: $70 per person
  • Semi-Private Price: $75 per person

A $200 deposit is required upon registration.

All lessons are taught by Equine Canada certified coaches who follow the Rider Level Program.
