★ About Claireville Ranch ★

Located just minutes north of the Greater Toronto Area in Brampton, Ontario, Claireville Ranch has been a beloved destination for horseback riding since 1983. Set on acres of beautiful natural forest within the Claireville Conservation Area, we offer a peaceful country escape where riders of all ages and skill levels can connect with horses and nature.

At Claireville Ranch, we provide a variety of experiences for every member of the family. From gentle pony rides for children to expert-led riding lessons following the Equine Canada Rider Level Program, we are committed to creating a safe, supportive, and fun environment. Our certified instructors are passionate about teaching and helping riders build confidence and skills in every lesson.

For those looking to explore, our scenic trail rides wind through serene forest landscapes, offering a perfect chance to unwind and enjoy nature on horseback. Horse owners can also take advantage of our boarding services and riders can join our welcoming Riders Club to become part of the Claireville community.

Beyond riding, Claireville Ranch is also a favorite spot for special occasions. Our picturesque grounds provide an ideal backdrop for unforgettable wedding photos and family celebrations.

Whether you're new to riding or a seasoned equestrian, we invite you, your family, and friends to experience the warm hospitality and natural beauty of Claireville Ranch. Come discover why we’ve been a treasured part of the Brampton community for over 40 years.

Note: Due to high demand and limited space, advance booking is recommended to ensure availability.
